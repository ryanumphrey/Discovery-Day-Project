Rosseau Lake College - Sailing Regatta

June 6, 2020 9:00AM - 4:00PM

Rosseau Lake College is a Co-ed Boarding/Day School on the shores of beautiful Lake Rosseau. As a place where friends become family, we invison this event as one to bring people together for exciting experiences on the water. This Regatta is one to allow people to try new things, to spend time with family, and to experience the way our way of life here at RLC. It is our hope that you will come out and join us in a day full of things to do, smiles to have and laughs to enjoy.

Event Info

The event will take place at the Rosseau
beach and the Rosseau Lake College campus.
The event will be free admission
however, donation boxes will be place
around the event. All money raised
in these donation boxes will be going back into
the sailing program at RLC for future generations of
people to enjoy. There will also be food placed
throughout the event, and sailing events taking place
constantly throughout the day.

Things To Do!

-Sailing Lessons
-Bouncy Castle
-Sailing Races
-Swimming Competitions
-Log Rolling
-Canoe Races

Local Community Impact

This event can benefit the local community
by getting people involved for a fun day
out on the water! This event
allows people to spend time with family
and to get outside and enjoy what Muskoka
and RLC has to offer. Sailing has proven
mental and physical health benefits to those that are involved,
it has been proven to improve mental health in participating
individuals. This Regatta will also bring
money into the local community, supporting local
businesses, and will help give more people
the opportunity to learn how to sail.
